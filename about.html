About AfroBite

Our Mission

AfroBite is dedicated to revolutionizing food delivery in Burkina Faso and across West Africa. We believe that everyone deserves access to convenient, reliable, and fast food delivery services. Our mission is to connect local communities with their favorite restaurants while supporting local businesses and creating economic opportunities for delivery drivers.

Our Vision

We envision a future where digital food delivery becomes an integral part of daily life in West Africa. By leveraging technology and local partnerships, we aim to build a trusted platform that makes ordering food as simple as a few taps on your phone. We're committed to expanding access to digital commerce while maintaining the highest standards of service quality and security.

Based in Burkina Faso

AfroBite is proudly based in Burkina Faso, built by people who understand the local market, culture, and needs of our community. We're not just another tech company—we're your neighbors, committed to serving our community with integrity and excellence.

Secure Digital Payments

We understand the importance of secure financial transactions. That's why AfroBite integrates with trusted payment providers like Orange Money and Wave, ensuring that your payments are processed safely and efficiently. We never store your full payment credentials, and all transactions are encrypted and secured through our payment partners.

Reliable Logistics

Our logistics network is designed for reliability and speed. We work closely with restaurants to ensure timely order preparation and partner with verified delivery drivers who are committed to bringing your food fresh and on time. Every order is tracked in real-time, so you always know where your meal is.

The AfroBite Team

Behind AfroBite is a dedicated team of professionals passionate about technology, food, and serving our community. The AfroBite Team works tirelessly to improve our platform, support our partners, and ensure the best possible experience for our customers, restaurants, and delivery drivers.

We're constantly innovating and adapting to meet the evolving needs of our users. Whether you're a customer looking for convenience, a restaurant seeking to expand your reach, or a driver looking for flexible work opportunities, AfroBite is here to support you.

Thank you for being part of the AfroBite community. Together, we're building the future of food delivery in West Africa.
