AfroBite

Fast. Local. Reliable.

AfroBite is Burkina Faso's premier food delivery platform, connecting hungry customers with local restaurants and dedicated delivery drivers. Experience the convenience of ordering your favorite meals from the comfort of your home or office, with secure digital payments and reliable delivery service.

How It Works

1

Customer

Browse restaurants, select your favorite meals, and place your order through our easy-to-use platform. Pay securely using Orange Money, Wave, or other supported payment methods.

2

Restaurant

Restaurants receive orders in real-time, prepare fresh meals, and confirm when ready. Our platform helps restaurants manage orders efficiently and grow their customer base.

3

Delivery

Our network of verified delivery drivers picks up your order and brings it directly to your location. Track your order in real-time and receive updates every step of the way.
