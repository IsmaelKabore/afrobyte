Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I place an order?

To place an order, download the AfroBite mobile app or visit our website, browse available restaurants, select your items, choose your delivery address, select a payment method (Orange Money, Wave, etc.), and confirm your order. You'll receive order confirmation and tracking updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

AfroBite accepts payments through Orange Money, Wave, and other authorized mobile money and payment services. All payments are processed securely through our trusted payment partners.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on restaurant preparation time, distance, and current order volume. Estimated delivery times are shown when you place your order. Typical delivery times range from 30-60 minutes, but may be longer during peak hours or adverse weather conditions.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can typically be cancelled before the restaurant begins preparation. Once preparation has started, cancellation may not be possible. To cancel an order, contact support immediately or use the cancellation option in your order details if available.

What if my order is incorrect or missing items?

If your order is incorrect or missing items, please contact support immediately at afrobyteapp@gmail.com with your order number. We will work with the restaurant to resolve the issue and may provide a refund or replacement as appropriate.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you can track its status in real-time through the AfroBite app or website. You'll receive updates when your order is being prepared, picked up by a driver, and out for delivery.

What delivery areas do you serve?

AfroBite currently serves designated areas within Burkina Faso. Delivery areas may vary by restaurant. When placing an order, you'll see available restaurants and delivery options for your location.

How do I update my account information?

You can update your account information, including delivery addresses, phone number, and email, through your account settings in the AfroBite app or by contacting support.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, AfroBite takes payment security seriously. We use trusted third-party payment processors (Orange Money, Wave, etc.) and do not store your full payment credentials. All transactions are encrypted and processed securely.

How do I become a restaurant partner?

If you're a restaurant interested in partnering with AfroBite, please contact us at afrobyteapp@gmail.com with "Restaurant Partnership" in the subject line. We'll provide information about our partnership program and requirements.

How do I become a delivery driver?

If you're interested in becoming a delivery driver for AfroBite, please contact us at afrobyteapp@gmail.com with "Delivery Driver Application" in the subject line. We'll provide information about driver requirements and the application process.

What should I do if I have a complaint?

We take all complaints seriously. Please contact us at afrobyteapp@gmail.com with detailed information about your complaint, including your order number (if applicable) and any relevant details. We will investigate and respond promptly.
